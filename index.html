Wakulla Springs 1st Magnitude

465 Wakulla Park Dr, Crawfordville, FL 32327

Springs are classified by magnitude (from one to eight) on the basis of their volume of flow, or discharge of water.

Magnitude 1 100 cubic feet per second or more

Magnitude 2 10 to 100 cubic feet per second

Magnitude 3 1 to 10 cubic feet per second

Magnitude 4 0.223 to 1 cubic feet per second

Magnitude 5 0.0223 to 0.223 cubic feet per second

Magnitude 6 0.0022 to 0.0223 cubic feet per second

Magnitude 7 0.00028 to 0.0022 cubic feet per second

Magnitude 8 0.00028 cubic feet per second or less
